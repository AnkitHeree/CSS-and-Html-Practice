Story of Civilization Recall

The Story of Civilization is an eleven-volume historical series by Will Durant and Ariel Durant (known as "the Durants") published between 1935 and 1975, covering the history of human civilization from ancient times to the Napoleonic era.

The series, which won the Pulitzer Prize for General Nonfiction in 1968 for the tenth volume (Rousseau and Revolution), spans over 11,000 pages and is celebrated for its comprehensive narrative of both Eastern and Western civilizations, with a particular emphasis on European history.
